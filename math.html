📘 Math Tutorials

Learn essential math concepts, from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus.

📌 Arithmetic

Arithmetic is the study of basic operations on numbers.

Addition: 5 + 3 = 8
Subtraction: 10 - 4 = 6
Multiplication: 7 × 2 = 14
Division: 20 ÷ 5 = 4

📌 Algebra

Algebra involves using symbols and letters to represent numbers in equations.

Equation: x + 2 = 5  →  x = 3
Expression: 3x + 4
Simplifying: 2(x + 3) = 2x + 6

📌 Geometry

Geometry studies shapes, sizes, and properties of space.

Triangle Area: A = ½ × base × height
Circle Area: A = π × r²
Pythagorean Theorem: a² + b² = c²

📌 Calculus

Calculus focuses on change and motion through derivatives and integrals.

Derivative: d/dx (x²) = 2x
Integral: ∫ x dx = (x²/2) + C

📌 Statistics

Statistics deals with data collection, analysis, and interpretation.

Mean (Average): (Σx) / n
Median: Middle value in an ordered set
Probability: P(A) = Favorable Outcomes / Total Outcomes
